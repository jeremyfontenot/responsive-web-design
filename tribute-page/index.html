Jesus of Nazareth

Key Facts

  • Born: 6 to 4 BC, Herodian kingdom, Roman Empire
  • Died: AD 30 or 33 (aged 33–38), Jerusalem, Judaea, Roman Empire
  • Cause of death: Crucifixion
  • Known for: Central figure of Christianity, Major prophet in Islam and the Druze Faith, Manifestation of God in the Bahá'í Faith
  • Parents: Mary, Joseph
